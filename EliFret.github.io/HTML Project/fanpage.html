About the MCU

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a shared universe centered on a series of superhero films produced by Marvel Studios[1]. It has become one of the most successful and popular film franchises in history, featuring iconic characters like Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor[1].

Popular MCU Characters

  • Iron Man
  • Captain America
  • Black Widow
  • Thor
  • Hulk
  • Spider-Man

MCU Phases

The MCU is divided into phases, each culminating in an Avengers movie[1]. Here's a brief overview:

  • Phase One: Introduced the core Avengers team
  • Phase Two: Expanded the universe with new heroes
  • Phase Three: Culminated in the epic Infinity Saga
  • Phase Four: Introduced the multiverse concept
